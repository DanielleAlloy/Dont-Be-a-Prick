Danielle has a BS in Computer Programming and Information Systems from Farmingdale State College, in Farmingdale, NY. She also has a BFA in Photography from the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York, NY. She has a real passion for the front end side of websites, due to her creative background. Her love of math and problem solving also caused her to fall in love with coding. She also loves to create mobile and desktop applications, and is fluent in JAVA, C#, Swift, PHP, JavaScript, C++, SQL, and others.

Danielle is available for freelance and comissioned work. Please see the "Contact Danielle" tab to get in touch.
