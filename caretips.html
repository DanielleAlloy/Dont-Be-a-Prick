Care Tips

Taking care of succulents and cacti can seem daunting at first, but once you get the hang of it, you will see it's easy.

Watering Tips

The most important part of caring for cacti and succulents is properly watering them. Too much water, and they will get root rot and die, too little water and they will shrivel up.
